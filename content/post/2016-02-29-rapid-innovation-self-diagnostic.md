**What&#8217;s preventing your ability to rapidly innovate?** 

Answer the following:

  1. How long does it take you to deliver even a single line of code change into production?
  2. How long does it take you to bring a new product to market?
  3. How often do you fail to meet commitments to both customers and business partners?
  4. Do you want to lower the cost of bringing your products to market?
  5. Do you have a deeper backlog of work than you have resources?
  6. What level of transparency does your process have?
  7. How much does support work interrupt your new product development?
  8. How does the quality of your products suffer because of all of the above?

I&#8217;d love to hear your responses!
